    <h1>Jollof Rice</h1>

    <p>
      This simple Jollof Rice recipe delivers fluffy, rich, tomato‑based rice with
      minimal fuss—perfect for weeknights or gatherings.
    </p>

    <h2>Ingredients</h2>
    <ul>
      <li>2 cups long‑grain rice (rinsed)</li>
      <li>1 cup tomato puree (or blended fresh tomatoes)</li>
      <li>1 medium onion, finely chopped</li>
      <li>2 tablespoons vegetable oil</li>
      <li>1 teaspoon curry powder</li>
      <li>1 teaspoon dried thyme</li>
      <li>1–2 cups chicken or vegetable stock</li>
      <li>Salt and pepper to taste</li>
    </ul>

    <h2>Instructions</h2>
    <ol>
      <li>Heat oil in a pot over medium heat; sauté onions until soft.</li>
      <li>Stir in tomato puree; simmer 5–7 minutes to reduce and deepen flavor.</li>
      <li>Add curry, thyme, salt, pepper, and stock; bring to a gentle boil.</li>
      <li>Stir in rinsed rice; reduce heat to low, cover, and cook until tender.</li>
      <li>Fluff with a fork; adjust seasoning and serve warm.</li>
    </ol>
